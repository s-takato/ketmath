KeTMath Rule   2023-07-08

  1. Fraction $\dfrac{a}{b}\Longleftrightarrow$ fr(a,b)　*)Small fraction tfr(a,b)
  2. Multiplication $ab \Longleftrightarrow$ ab
  3. Exponentiation $a^b \Longleftrightarrow$ a^(b)
  4. Power Root $\sqrt{a},\sqrt[3]{a} \Longleftrightarrow$ sq(a),sq(3,a)
  5. Trigonometric Function $\sin x,\sin^2x\Longleftrightarrow$ sin(x),sin(2,x) or sin(x)^(2)
  6. Degree $60{}^{\circ}\Longleftrightarrow$ 60(deg)
  7. Pi $\pi \Longleftrightarrow$ pi
  8. Logarithmic Function $\log x,\log_a x,\ln x \Longleftrightarrow$ log(x),log(a,x),ln(x)
  9. New Line $\Longleftrightarrow$ //
  10. Space $\Longleftrightarrow$ (sp)　*) \; is output
  11. Roman $100\text{m}\Longleftrightarrow$ 100tx(m)
  12. Integral $\displaystyle\int x^2 dx,\int_a^b x^2dx \Longleftrightarrow$ int(x^2,x),int(a,b,x^2,x)
    *）Put ! on the head to remove parentheses
  13. Bracket $\Bigl[f(x)\Bigr]_a^b \Longleftrightarrow$ br(f(x),a,b)
  14. Limit $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to a}f(x) \Longleftrightarrow$ lim(x,a,f(x))

  15. Sum $\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^2 \Longleftrightarrow$ sum(k=1,n,k^2)

  16. Diffentiation $\dfrac{dy}{dx},\ \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x} \Longleftrightarrow$ diff(y,x), par(z,x)

  17. Matrix $\begin{pmatrix}a&b\\c&d\end{pmatrix},\left|\begin{array}{cc}a&b\\c&d\end{array}\right| \Longleftrightarrow$ mat(a,b;c,d),det(a,b;c,d)

  18. Case $\begin{cases}a&(x<0)\\c&(x \geq 0)\end{cases} \ \Longleftrightarrow$ case(a,(x<0);c,(x(geq)0))
  19. dot $\cdot\ \cdots,\ \times \Longrightarrow$ cdot() cdot(s) (cross)
  20. Double Sign $\pm,\mp\ \Longleftrightarrow$ (pm),(mp)
  21. Inequality $<,>,\leq,\geq,=\hspace*{-3mm}/ \Longleftrightarrow$ <,>,(leq),(geq),(neq)
  22. Under Score $a_n \Longleftrightarrow$ a_n
  23. Greek $\alpha,\beta \Longleftrightarrow$ {\alpha},{\beta}
  24. Other TeX Symbols $\Longrightarrow$ {\overline},\ ...
